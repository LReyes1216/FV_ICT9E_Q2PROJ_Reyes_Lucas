ABOUT SKIBS INC.

From a school project, to a worldwide business, Skibs has grown! We started as young high schoolers doing a business project for school. With determination, we transformed our dreams into a reality. And we want to share our story, so you can do it too!




HISTORY OF SKIBS INC.

  • On December 18, 2023, a group of young fellows decided to share a dream, to own a business. They later settled on a food business named “Skibs.”
  • On January 18, 2024, they decided to branch out Skibs by creating a sister company called Pastee. Pastee specialized on energizing the customers while making them satisfied.
  • On August 9, 2024, they decided to surprise one of the founders (because it was his birthday). The Skibs Inc. team decided to open its first restaurant outside Southeast Asia.
