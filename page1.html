Tapsilog

Ingredients
Tapa and Assembly
  • 1lb. sirloin, thinly sliced against the grain into bite-size strips
  • 6 garlic cloves, coarsely chopped
  • ⅓ cup (packed) light brown sugar
  • ¼ cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp. distilled white vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp. cornstarch
  • ½ tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
  • ½ tsp. freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp. kosher salt
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 4 cups chilled leftover cooked jasmine rice
  • 1 tsp. Diamond Crystal or ½ tsp. Morton kosher salt, plus more
  • ¼ cup (or more) vegetable oil
  • 12 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 4 large eggs

Procedure

  1. Pat the beef dry. In a medium bowl, whisk garlic, brown sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, cornstarch, red pepper flakes, black pepper, and salt. After that, add beef to the marinade and toss it well. Cover and chill it for at least 6 hours and up to 24 hours.
  2. Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over high. Drain beef, letting as much excess marinade off as possible. Add half of the beef to the skillet, arranging in a single layer and cook, turning halfway through, until beef is cooked through and deep brown in color, about 3 minutes. Transfer beef to a plate. Remove any garlic remaining in pan and repeat the process. Keep warm. Clean and set aside.
  3. Place rice in a large bowl and break up with a rubber spatula. Stir in 1 tsp. Diamond Crystal or 1/2 tsp. Morton kosher salt. After that, set it aside.
  4. Heat oil in reserved skillet over medium-low. Cook the garlic, stirring occasionally, until golden brown in color, about 5 minutes. Drain the garlic over a small bowl; transfer to a small plate.
  5. Pour 2 Tbsp. garlic oil back into the skillet and heat over medium-high. Add the rice mixture and stir to coat in oil. Cook until heated through, about 5 minutes. Set 1 Tbsp. fried garlic aside for serving and stir remaining fried garlic into rice. Transfer to a bowl and keep it warm.
  6. Heat remaining garlic oil over medium-high. Crack eggs into the skillet and season. Cook until whites are almost set, about 2 minutes. Carefully tilt skillet so oil goes on one side. Spoon oil over egg whites until fully cooked, about 1 minute.
  7. Plate and enjoy!

Tocilog

Ingredients
Tocino and Assembly
  • 1 batch Sinangag (garlic rice)
  • 160 grams Tocino
  • 4 eggs

Procedure

  1. The Tocino can be warm or cold and takes the most time, so we usually put it in a cold non-stick pan and cook it over medium heat until it starts to caramelize around the meat.
  2. Start the Sinangag as soon as you have the Tocino going.
  3. Place rice in a large bowl and break up with a rubber spatula. Stir in 1 tsp. Diamond Crystal or 1/2 tsp. Morton kosher salt. After that, set it aside.
  4. If you have enough pans, you can start the eggs already. If you don't, you can make them in the same pan as the garlic rice, after it's done and you've plated it.
  5. Plate everything and enjoy!
