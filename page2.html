Beef Kaldereta

Ingredients
Kaldereta
  • 2 lbs beef cubed
  • 3 pieces garlic cloves crushed and chopped
  • 1 piece onion finely chopped
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 piece red bell pepper sliced
  • 1 piece green bell pepper sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • ½ cup liver spread processed using blender
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 2 cups potatoes sliced
  • 2 cups carrots sliced
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • ⅔ cup green olives
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. Heat the cooking oil in your cooking pot or pressure cooker.
  2. Sauté the onion and garlic.
  3. After you sauté the onion and garlic, add the beef. Cook it for 5 minutes or until light brown in color.
  4. Add dried bay leaves and chili flakes or crushed pepper after cooking the beef. Stir it well.
  5. Add your liver spread to taste. Stir well.
  6. Pour-in the tomato sauce and beef broth. After pouring it in, cook the beef until it is tender. Make sure you add the potato and carrots after this. Cook it for 8 to 10 minutes.
  7. Put the green olives and bell peppers in the cooking pot. Stir and continue to cook for 5 more minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste, and enjoy!

Sinigang

Ingredients
Sinigang
  • 2 lbs. pork belly
  • 1 lb. young tamarind
  • 1 bunch water spinach chopped
  • 8 pieces string beans cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 pieces eggplants sliced
  • 1 piece daikon radish
  • 8 pieces okras
  • 2 pieces tomatoes sliced into wedges
  • 2 pieces long green pepper
  • 1 piece onion sliced into wedges
  • 2 quarts water
  • Fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. Boil the young tamarind in 2 quarts of water for 40 minutes. Filter the tamarind broth, then squeeze the tamarind afterwards to extract its remaining juices.
  2. Pour the tamarind broth into a cooking pot. Let it boil and then add the onion, pork belly, and 1/2 of the tomatoes.
  3. When you see floating scums, skim it off, then pour 1 tablespoon fish sauce. Cover and continue to simmer for 1 hour.
  4. Add daikon radish and eggplants. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. After 5 minutes, add the long green pepper, string beans, remaining tomatoes, and okra. Cook for 3 minutes.
  6. Add the chopped water spinach stalks and season with fish sauce and ground black pepper (to taste). Cook for 2 minutes. After 2 minutes, put the water spinach leaves. Cover and turn the heat off. Let the heat cook the leaves for 3 minutes before serving. Plate and enjoy!
