Dessert Recipes


Leche flan

Ingredients
Leche flan
  • 10 pieces eggs
  • 1 can condensed milk (14 oz)
  • 1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Procedure

  1. Using all the eggs, use the egg yolks only. Place the egg yolks in a big bowl then beat them. Add the condensed milk and mix thoroughly. Pour-in the fresh milk and vanilla. Mix well
  2. Put the mold (llanera) on top of the stove and heat using low fire.
  3. Put-in the granulated sugar on the mold and mix until liquid (caramel) having a light brown color. If you can't find a mold, you may use round pans.
  4. Spread the caramel (liquid sugar) evenly on the flat side of the mold. Wait for 5 minutes then pour the egg yolk and milk mixture. Cover the top using aluminum foil.
  5. Steam it for 30 to 35 minutes. After steaming, let the temperature cool down then refrigerate. Serve for dessert. Share and Enjoy!

Puto

Ingredients
Puto
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour or cake flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • One tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 cup evaporated milk
  • 3/4 cup water
  • One tablespoon of melted butter
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (optional)
  • Strips of cheese for toppings
  • One egg

Procedure

  1. Get a bowl and sift the flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Get another bowl and add egg, melted butter, evaporated milk, and water. Mix well. After that, combine this with the dry mixture.
  3. You must mix it carefully, or else there will be lumps. Add grated cheese once done, and mix it again. Let the batter rest for up to 15 minutes. Pour the batter into your molds or containers and add cheese if you wish.
  4. Prepare a steamer and place it inside; let them cook for 10 minutes. Use a lid with a thick cloth to prevent steam water from dripping.
  5. After 10 minutes, take it out and enjoy!
